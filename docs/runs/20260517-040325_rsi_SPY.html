rsi on SPY

Mean reversion driven by Wilder's Relative Strength Index. When RSI drops into oversold territory (default below 30) we go fully invested; when it climbs into overbought (default above 70) we move back to 100% cash. Tends to do well in choppy, range-bound markets and to lag badly during sustained bull runs because it keeps selling into strength. By default this strategy starts already invested on day one; pass start_in_cash=true to wait for the first oversold signal.
